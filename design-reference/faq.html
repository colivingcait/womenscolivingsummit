FAQ

Questions? We've got answers.

Everything you need to know about the Women's Coliving Summit — from tickets to dress code to what happens if your plans change.

When and where is WCS 2026?

+
WCS 2026 takes place October 16–17 in Atlanta, Georgia. The venue will be announced this summer — follow us on social media or sign up for updates so you're the first to know.

Who should attend?

+
WCS is for women at every stage of the coliving journey. Whether you're still exploring the model and running your first numbers, building systems in your first property, scaling a portfolio across markets, or you work in a field that supports coliving — real estate, lending, furnishing, property management, technology — there's a seat here for you.

Is this event open to men and women?

+
The Women's Coliving Summit is intentionally designed as a space for women. We believe that when women have a room built specifically for them — to learn, connect, and grow without filtering — something powerful happens. It's not about exclusion. It's about creating the conditions for women to show up fully and build boldly.

How much are tickets?

+
Ticket pricing and tiers are available on our ticketing page. View tickets and pricing here.

Is there a virtual option?

+
Yes! We'll have a virtual attendee option this year as well. Virtual tickets include live access to the sessions plus full video recordings of both days so you can revisit everything at your own pace.

What's included with my ticket?

+
Your ticket includes access to all sessions across both days — presentations, panels, workshops, and breakout sessions — plus intentional networking time, lunches, light snacks and refreshments throughout the day, and video recordings of all sessions. In-person attendees also receive membership to the She Leads Coliving community.

Is there a hotel room block?

+
Yes — a hotel room block will be available and announced alongside the venue details this summer. We'll make sure you have a convenient, comfortable option close to the action.

What should I wear?

+
Business attire always looks great at conferences — dress to meet your future business partners and to show up as the best version of you. And if you're a jumpsuit kind of girl, so are we. ;)

Can I get a refund?

+
Due to the nature of live events — venue deposits, catering headcounts, and production planning — we're unable to offer refunds on tickets. However, we're happy to help you transfer your ticket to a friend or colleague. And if you can't find someone, don't worry — as the event approaches, we'll open a ticket donation program that provides sponsored tickets to women in the real estate investing field who want to attend but may not have the means to yet. Last year, we were proud to welcome three Kennesaw State University students on scholarship through this program.

How do I become a sponsor?

+
We love our sponsors — they're an essential part of what makes WCS possible. We'd love to have a conversation about our sponsorship packages and find the right fit for your brand. Click here to submit a sponsor inquiry.

How do I apply to speak?

+
We're always looking for women with real coliving experience and a perspective worth sharing — operators, investors, and industry leaders who can teach, inspire, and equip our attendees. If that sounds like you, we'd love to hear from you. Click here to submit a speaker application.

I have another question — how do I reach you?

+
We'd love to hear from you! Reach out anytime at Hello@LustraHouse.com and we'll get back to you as soon as we can.
